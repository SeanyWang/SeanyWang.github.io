Sean H. Wang

Starting on May 15, 2017, I will be a Project Manager and Research Scholar at the Max Planck Institute for the History of Science in Berlin, Germany. I am currently a PhD Candidate in Geography and a National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellow at the Maxwell School of Syracuse University. Working with Jamie Winders, my dissertation research looks at the politics of immigration and birthright citizenship policies, via a comparative ethnography of local responses to Chinese birth tourism in Hong Kong and in southern California, respectively.
